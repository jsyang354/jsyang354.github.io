Sports

It's all about "SPORTS". Thanks to my dad, who used to be a high school P.E. teacher in Korea, sports were always around me. Until now, sports have been a part of my life, and I like to do sports-related activities such as watching, playing, and going to a game whenever I have time or get stressed out!

Soccer

Among all ball games, soccer is my most favorite one. I love watching and playing both computer and actual game! I have been playing soccer since elementary school, and now I enjoy playing soccer with my teammates here in UIUC as well.

Baseball

Baseball was the first ball game I have ever played and also one of my favorite sports. There was a ballpark near my home and I often went there with my dad. Watching 'Moneyball' was one of the game changers in my life that made me to have an interest in sports and statistics (sabermetrics).

Basketball

Although I have recently started to watch basketball, it is somewhat more exciting to watch the game than I expected. The atmosphere that I have experienced in the stadium was new, and the overall game tempo is much faster than soccer or baseball, makes me more thrilled!
